PROFESSIONAL PRINTING SPECIFICATIONS
=====================================

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Color Mode: CMYK (4-color process)
- Resolution: 300 DPI minimum
- File Format: PDF/X-1a or High Quality Print PDF
- Fonts: All fonts embedded
- Images: 300 DPI, CMYK color space

BLEED AND MARGINS:
- Bleed: 0.125" (3mm) on all sides
- Safe Area: 0.25" (6mm) from trim edge
- Fold Lines: Scored before folding for clean creases

PAPER RECOMMENDATIONS:
- Tri-fold Brochure: 100-120 GSM coated paper (gloss or matte)
- Bi-fold Brochure: 150-200 GSM coated paper for premium feel
- Single Sheet: 120-150 GSM coated paper

FINISHING OPTIONS:
- UV Coating: Recommended for durability and professional appearance
- Folding: Machine folding with scoring for sharp creases
- Quantities: 250+ for best per-unit pricing

QUALITY CONTROL:
- Request proof print before full run
- Check color accuracy against brand standards
- Verify all text is readable and properly aligned
- Ensure contact information is accurate

DELIVERY NOTES:
- Allow 3-5 business days for printing
- Request extra copies (10% overage) for events
- Consider rush options if needed for tight deadlines

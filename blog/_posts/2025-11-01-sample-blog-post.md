### The 2025 Harvest

The autumn harvest season has arrived in Kakheti! Our vineyards are alive with activity as we carefully select and hand-harvest our Saperavi and Rkatsiteli grapes.

**This year's highlights:**
- Perfect weather conditions in September
- Exceptional sugar content in the grapes
- Traditional qvevri preparation underway
- New oak barrels from France arriving soon

### Behind the Scenes

Our winemaking team has been preparing the cellars for the new vintage. The ancient qvevri vessels have been cleaned and prepared according to traditional Georgian methods, passed down through generations.

### What's Next

Stay tuned for updates on the fermentation process and our plans for the 2025 vintage!
